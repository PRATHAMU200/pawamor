Frequently Asked Questions

How do I submit my pet story?

Use the contact form above and select "Submit a Story" as the subject. Share your story in the message field - we'd love to hear about your special bond with your pet!

Can you provide veterinary advice?

While we share general pet care information, we cannot provide specific veterinary advice. For health concerns, always consult with a licensed veterinarian.

How long does it take to get a response?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please contact your local veterinarian or emergency animal hospital.

Do you accept guest blog posts?

Yes! We welcome contributions from pet professionals and experienced pet owners. Contact us with "Partnership Inquiry" and tell us about your proposed topic.
